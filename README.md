# Telco-Customer-Churn-Business-Analytics
📊 **Telco Customer Churn Business Analytics Case Study**

PROJECT INTERVIEW

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges facing telecommunication companies. Understanding why customers leave enables organizations to develop effective retention strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term profitability.

In this project, I analyzed the Telco Customer Churn Dataset to identify the major drivers of customer churn and provide actionable business recommendations using SQL Server and Microsoft Excel.

⸻

BUSINESS PROBLEM

The company is experiencing customer churn, resulting in customer loss and reduced revenue.

This analysis seeks to answer the following business questions:

* Why are customers leaving the company?
* Which factors contribute most to customer churn?
* Which customer segments require immediate attention?
* What strategies can improve customer retention?

⸻

* Source: Kaggle (IBM Sample Dataset)

KEY FINDINGS

* Customer churn rate is 26.54%.
* Month-to-month contracts recorded the highest churn.
* Electronic Check customers were more likely to churn.
* Fiber Optic customers experienced higher churn than other internet service types.
* Customers without Technical Support had significantly higher churn.
* Customers paying higher monthly charges tended to churn earlier.

⸻
REOMMENDATIONS

* Encourage customers to adopt longer-term contracts.
* Promote automatic payment methods.
* Improve Fiber Optic customer experience.
* Increase Technical Support adoption.
* Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
